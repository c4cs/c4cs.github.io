comm
---
Compares two sorted files line by line and writes three columns to standard output.
<!-- one line explanation would go here -->

<!-- minimal example -->
~~~ bash
$ cat file1.txt
abc
def
ghi

$ cat file2.txt
abc
ghi
klm

$ comm file1.txt file2.txt
		abc
def
		ghi
	klm
~~~
